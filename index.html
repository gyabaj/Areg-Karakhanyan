My Way From Art To Programming!

Musicians are natural-born Programmers. Both professions deal with abstract concepts. In addition, musicians and programmers conceptualize their media in a hierarchical organization. When musicians think about Beethoven's Fifth Symphony, they see it as a singular work that is divided into different movements, each of which is further divided into sections, phrases, bars, and single notes. Programmers think about their programs in a similar manner. The words in an individual line of program code correspond to the notes on a page of music. Programs even have repeats and first and second endings in the form of loops and cases.
